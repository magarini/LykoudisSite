Projects

Pantelis is a saxophonist, educator and improviser with various research interests. As a chamber musician, he has performed a wide range of repertoire, in addition to new works by composers such as Harold Groenenboom and Xander Benham, among others. His projects include a trio performing music of Anthony Braxton and Utrecht Experimental Ensemble .
